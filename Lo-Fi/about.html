About Lo-Fi Music

History of Lo-Fi

Lo-fi music originated from the DIY recording practices of the 1980s and 1990s, where artists used affordable equipment, resulting in a raw sound. This genre gained traction as it embraced the beauty of imperfections.

Lo-Fi in Modern Culture

Lo-fi music has seen a resurgence, particularly as background music for studying and relaxation. It is popular on platforms like YouTube and Spotify, with channels dedicated to streaming lo-fi beats 24/7.

Creating Your Own Lo-Fi Music

Interested in making your own lo-fi beats? Start with basic recording equipment, embrace imperfections, and focus on creating a relaxed, nostalgic vibe. Experiment with samples, vinyl crackle, and simple melodies.
